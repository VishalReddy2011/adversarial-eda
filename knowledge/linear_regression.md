Simple linear regression

Use when one numeric predictor is used to explain or predict one numeric target.

Assumptions:
- at least 3 paired observations
- predictor and target are non-constant
- relationship is approximately linear
- residual assumptions should be considered before making strong claims

Report slope, intercept, p_value, r_squared, and n. Treat a low r_squared as weak explanatory power even if p_value is significant.
